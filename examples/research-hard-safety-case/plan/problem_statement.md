# Hard Research Problem

Design a multi-agent AI research workflow to produce a jailbreak-resilient long-context LLM agent under adaptive adversarial attacks, while preserving task utility and controlling inference cost.

## Why this is hard
- Adaptive attacks evolve after each defense patch.
- Long-context settings create hidden prompt injection channels.
- Safety, utility, and latency are conflicting objectives.
- Evidence quality and reproducibility often degrade under fast iteration.
