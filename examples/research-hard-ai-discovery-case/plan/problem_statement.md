# Hard AI Research Problem

Design a multi-agent scientific discovery system that identifies robust causal biomarkers for early sepsis prediction from multi-omics + EHR data under severe domain shift.

## Constraints
- Clinical safety first: false negative risk must be tightly controlled.
- Reproducibility: all critical claims must be traceable to tool evidence.
- Domain generalization: performance must remain stable across hospitals and time windows.
- Decision transparency: each final recommendation must expose uncertainty and rationale.

## Success Criteria
- Strong predictive quality under distribution shift.
- Calibrated uncertainty and low hallucination behavior.
- Actionable intervention recommendations with reviewer-approved evidence.
